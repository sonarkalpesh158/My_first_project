Who we are

Samarth Clinic is a professionally managed facility offering modern medical solutions & accessible to any one in need of any kind of medical attention. A humble beginning was made Lonkheda town where the clinic started to cater to the medical needs of the people of North Maharashtra. The untiring efforts and selfless service received overwhelming encouragement and was widely appreciated. This was the beginning of the journey of Samarth Clinic. Over these years, the clinic has emerged into a credible name dedicated to the health care and well being of the society. This clinic provides all modern medical facilities under one roof & provides the best medical treatments and services at reasonable cost. The clinic are complemented with modern facilities and advanced medical equipment. The hospital has developed a sound infrastructure keeping in mind the future growth and capabilities. Our staff is motivated to lend a human touch to each patient and thus our institution is called as "Clinic with Human Touch". The Faith and blessings of the people has helped us in continuously modernizing the clinic and improve the services. The hospital has undertaken an number of projects in the field of social work. The clinic is committed to ensure the mental and physical well being of all patients - thus taking both emotional and professional approach for treating them.
